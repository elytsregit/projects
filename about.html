About Sammie's Sports Memorabilia!

We are Culver Springs number one destination for all your sports collectibles and memorabilia needs. Sammie's Sports Memorabilia opened in 1993 in downtown Culver Springs. Since that moment, we have been serving the greater Culver Springs area and providing our own brand of excellent service. Sammie's Sports Memorabilia specializes in the buying and selling of sports memorabilia as well as the appraising of sports collectibles. We may be from a small town, but we have numerous connections to many authenticators around the country that we work with to accurately appraise your sports collectibles.

From an early age, Sammie King (pictured above) was obsessed with sports. He would save up all his allowance from mowing lawns around the neighborhood just to buy small packs of trading cards in hopes of finding a "big score". The first big item that Sammie discovered was a Kareem Abdul-Jabbar rookie card that he still has to this day. It is the piece that started this obsession that eventually turned into a business.

Whether you are looking for Hockey, Football, Soccer, Baseball, Basketball, Tennis, Golf, or even Olympic Sport memorabilia. Look no further than Sammie's Sports Memorabilia in Culver Springs, AR!
