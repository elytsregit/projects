Welcome all!


Hey hey citizens of Mount Vista! It is that time again where our local talent show is just around the corner on December 28th, 2019. Our local talent show is one of the biggest events that our small community has for the year, and we are thinking that this year will be the biggest and best ever. As you may know, anyone is eligible to sign-up for the talent show so feel free to head over to our "How to Sign-Up" page for more information. This year, we are pleased to announce that we are going to have free catering this year sponsored by Pete's Hickory BBQ. Make sure you let Pete know how much we appreciate his generosity this year and do not forget to stop by Pete's Hickory BBQ for one of the best food gems of Mount Vista. If you have any questions about this year's event, head on over to our "Contact Us" page and shoot us some questions via E-mail, phone call, or text message.
