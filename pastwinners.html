Mount Vista Talent Show Bragging Corner

Hi folks! Here in Mount Vista, we are so proud of our community and would love to share a video of one of our most popular past acts, Gristle. Their hardwork led them to our talent show, which gave them the courage to keep pursuing their dream which eventually led them to the success of touring and signing a record deal. Unfortunately, this same success has also made it so we can not legally post the audio from this clip on our website, but the message still stays the same and you can just see the level of performance these guys are putting on now. Here in Mount Vista, we support the citizens of our community and love to see the passions from our talent shows extend into real jobs and careers.
