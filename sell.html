Sammie's Buys Your Memorabilia!

We are always interested in appraising and buying your sports memorabilia. Just call us up and make an appointment for an appraisal and we will find the right expert that can accurately appraise your memorabilia. After the appraisal, we can negotiate pricing based upon the feedback we receive from the appraiser and the actual market for the item. These are currently the types of items we are looking for:

Baseball -- Cards, Jerseys, Pennants, Signed/Game-Used Equipment (Bats, Gloves, Shoes, etc.)

Basketball -- Cards, Jerseys, Signed/Game-Used Equipment (Balls, Sweatbands, Sleeves, Shoes, etc.)

Football -- Cards, Jerseys, Signed/Game-Used Equipment (Helmets, Balls, Shoes, etc.)

Hockey -- Cards, Jerseys, Signed/Game-Used Equipment (Helmets, Pucks, Sticks, Skates, etc.)

Soccer -- Cards, Soccer Kits, Signed/Game-Used Equipment (Cleats, Balls, etc.)

Tennis -- Jerseys, Signed/Game-Used Equipment (Rackets, Balls, Sweatbands, Shoes, etc.)

Golf -- Signed/Game-Used Equipment (Clubs, Balls, Shoes, etc.)

Olympics -- Any memorabilia

If you have any of these items and are interested in selling them, stop by our store or give us a call. If you have other sports memorabilia or collectibles that are not listed here, let us know and we might still be interested or can point you to someone who is. We are especially interested in autographed or game-used items. As always, feel free to contact us through the information on our "Contact Us" page.
